[OBJECT:ENTITY]

===========================================================================================================
Dark Ages: Chapter II: War & Mythos | 0.42.06 | R2 | GM-X | DFDarkAge.com | AdventRPG.com
===========================================================================================================

These civs can be avoided in fortress mode by embarking in location they haven't reached.

All civs build roads, bridges, tombs, fortresses and most build tunnels. This is a great help for world
exploration in adventure mode and civ interactions in world gen. 

============================================================================================================
